# Card Modifications

## 1. Enhancements
- **Bonus Card**: +30 Chips.
- **Mult Card**: +4 Mult.
- **Wild Card**: Can be any suit.
- **Glass Card**: x2 Mult, 1 in 4 chance to break.
- **Steel Card**: x1.5 Mult while in hand.
- **Stone Card**: +50 Chips, no rank or suit.
- **Gold Card**: $3 if in hand at end of round.
- **Lucky Card**: 1 in 5 for +20 Mult, 1 in 15 for $20.

## 2. Editions
- **Foil**: +50 Chips.
- **Holographic**: +10 Mult.
- **Polychrome**: x1.5 Mult.
- **Negative**: +1 Joker slot.

## 3. Seals
- **Red Seal**: Retrigger this card 1 time.
- **Blue Seal**: Create a Planet card of the last played hand (must be in hand at end of round).
- **Gold Seal**: $5 if this card is played and scores.
- **Purple Seal**: Create a Tarot card when discarded.
